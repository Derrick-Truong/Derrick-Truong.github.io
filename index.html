Combined with my immense curiosity in web-development and its ever expanding role in society, I knew my calling and aspiring career choice lay in the tech industry. Throughout my life, I have consistently been exposed to coding and web-application from my step-father. As a side-project, my step-father constructed a website centered around a course he created that helped guide people on creating start-up businesses. Every 1-2 months, I would ask for an update on the development of his website. He would show me certain codes and the functionalities of it. Although I did not understand the language and syntax of the code, I was fascinated by how these lines of numbers, letters, and symbols could be the backbone of so many functionalities of his website. Though the logical choice would have been to pursue a degree in software engineering when the time came to apply for colleges, I thought getting a degree in economics would be more practical. Fast forward to a few months after graduating and working at a small accounting firm, I quickly realized my skills and passion were not suited for the financial industry. I had a brief conversation with my step-father about my predicament of a career choice and he wisely suggested that I do some research about coding boot camps, recalling all the past interactions we shared about his website. After extensive research, I enrolled in the well-known App Academy program. For the past year, it has been a rigorous and enlightening experience that has cemented my choice of becoming a web developer. Though I do not have a traditional educational background as a web-developer, the choice to pivot career paths after receiving a degree in a completely different field demonstrates my dedication and genuine passion as a web-developer. Everyday I strive to expand and reinforce my knowledge in coding to help expand on the infinite potential of website development and designing. If you have any questions or want to get in contact, I am always available at my email provided at the bottom of the page.
